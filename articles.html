Article #3

The article “Comprehensive Incident Report Plan for Wellness Technology” presented a comprehensive incident report plan (IRP) for Wellness Technology. It addresses some of the technological potential threat to businesses, for example, malicious code or virus that compromises the Wellness Technology’s Web App or events such as fire. Furthermore, it outlines how the workers should report such events for proper action from the management. Once the reporting of an event has been done, the appropriate Information Technology department member is informed for response. The paper is relevant as it outlines the role of Information Technology department members, for example, executing the initial investigation with the aim of determining if an event has occurred. Notably, it highlights the steps which a company ought to take in conducting investigations and provides businesses with the recovery of such things as malware or malicious codes as well as fire. It also educates businesses on the benefits of regular trainings in ensuring effective response to the aforementioned events.
